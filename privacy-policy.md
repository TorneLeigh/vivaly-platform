# Privacy Policy

*Effective Date: June 3, 2025*
*Last Updated: June 3, 2025*

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Care Platform Australia Pty Ltd ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our care marketplace platform and related services (collectively, the "Services"). 

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and other applicable privacy laws.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide to Us

**Account Information**: When you register, we collect your name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, and password.

**Profile Information**: For caregivers, this includes detailed biographical information, experience, qualifications, certifications, skills, availability, hourly rates, and profile photos. For families, this includes household information, care needs, and preferences.

**Identity Verification**: Government-issued ID, Working with Children Check, police check certificates, first aid certifications, and other background verification documents.

**Payment Information**: Credit card details, billing addresses, and payment history (processed securely through Stripe - we do not store full payment card details).

**Communication Data**: All messages, reviews, and communications sent through our platform between users.

**Booking Information**: Service requests, booking details, schedules, special instructions, and payment transactions.

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

**Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform, search queries, and interaction patterns.

**Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and mobile network information.

**Location Data**: Approximate location based on IP address and precise location if you grant permission for location services.

**Cookies and Tracking**: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and analyze platform usage.

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

**Background Check Providers**: Criminal history, identity verification, and professional reference information from authorized screening services.

**Social Media**: If you connect social media accounts, we may receive basic profile information in accordance with your privacy settings.

**Payment Processors**: Transaction information and payment verification from Stripe and other payment partners.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Platform Operations
- Create and maintain your account
- Facilitate connections between families and caregivers
- Process payments and manage transactions
- Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
- Verify identity and conduct background checks

### 3.2 Safety and Security
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and illegal activities
- Conduct safety screenings and background verification
- Monitor platform activity for suspicious behavior
- Protect the safety of children, elderly, and pets in care arrangements

### 3.3 Communication
- Send booking confirmations, payment receipts, and service updates
- Facilitate messaging between platform users
- Send marketing communications about relevant services (with your consent)
- Provide important safety alerts and platform notifications

### 3.4 Improvement and Analytics
- Analyze platform usage to improve features and user experience
- Conduct research and development for new services
- Generate aggregate statistics and insights (anonymized)
- Personalize content and recommendations

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 With Your Consent
We share information with your explicit consent, such as when you choose to share your profile with potential clients or agree to background check sharing.

### 4.2 Public Information
Your caregiver profile (excluding sensitive personal details) is visible to registered families seeking care services. This includes your name, photo, experience, qualifications, and reviews.

### 4.3 Service Providers
We share information with trusted third parties who provide services on our behalf:
- **Payment processors** (Stripe) for transaction processing
- **Background check providers** for identity and safety verification
- **Cloud hosting services** (secure data storage)
- **Email service providers** for transactional communications
- **Analytics providers** for platform improvement

### 4.4 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by law, including:
- Court orders, subpoenas, or legal process
- Government investigations or regulatory requests
- Child protection or safety concerns
- Prevention of fraud or illegal activities

### 4.5 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred to the acquiring entity with appropriate privacy protections.

### 4.6 Emergency Situations
We may share information without consent in emergency situations to protect the safety of users, children, or the public.

## 5. Data Security and Protection

### 5.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256
- **Access Controls**: Multi-factor authentication and role-based access restrictions
- **Regular Security Audits**: Penetration testing and vulnerability assessments
- **Secure Infrastructure**: SOC 2 compliant cloud hosting with 24/7 monitoring

### 5.2 Organizational Measures
- **Staff Training**: Regular privacy and security training for all employees
- **Data Minimization**: We collect only necessary information for stated purposes
- **Retention Limits**: Personal data is deleted when no longer needed
- **Incident Response**: Formal procedures for security breach notification and remediation

### 5.3 Background Check Security
Identity verification documents and background check results are stored with enhanced security measures and access restricted to authorized personnel only.

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

### 6.1 Access and Portability
- Request access to all personal information we hold about you
- Receive a copy of your data in a portable format
- Review how your information is being used and shared

### 6.2 Correction and Updates
- Update or correct inaccurate personal information
- Modify your profile, preferences, and account settings
- Request correction of background check information

### 6.3 Deletion Rights
- Delete your account and associated personal data
- Request removal of specific information (subject to legal requirements)
- Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable

### 6.4 Communication Preferences
- Opt out of marketing emails and promotional communications
- Control notification settings and frequency
- Manage cookie preferences through browser settings

### 6.5 Complaint Rights
- Lodge complaints with the Australian Privacy Commissioner
- Contact our Data Protection Officer for privacy concerns
- Request investigation of privacy practice violations

## 7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Australia where our service providers operate. We ensure adequate protection through:
- **Standard Contractual Clauses** for GDPR compliance
- **Privacy Shield frameworks** where applicable
- **Adequacy decisions** by relevant data protection authorities
- **Binding Corporate Rules** for internal data transfers

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it immediately and notify relevant authorities if required.

## 9. Retention and Deletion

### 9.1 Account Information
Active accounts: Retained while your account is active and for legitimate business purposes.

### 9.2 Inactive Accounts
Accounts inactive for 2+ years may be deleted after 60 days notice.

### 9.3 Background Checks
Verification documents are retained for 7 years as required by Australian regulations.

### 9.4 Communication Records
Messages and booking history retained for 3 years for safety and dispute resolution.

### 9.5 Payment Information
Transaction records retained for 7 years for tax and accounting compliance.
